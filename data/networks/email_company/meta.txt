https://networks.skewed.de/net/email_company

A network of emails among employee email addresses at a mid-sized manufacturing company. Each directed edge represents an email sent from address i to address j. Edges are timestamped, and occurred over a 6 month period in an unspecified year.

R. Michalski, S. Palus, and P. Kazienko, "Matching organizational structure and social network extracted from email communication." In Lecture Notes in Business Information Processing 87, 197-206 (2011), https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-642-21863-7_17
