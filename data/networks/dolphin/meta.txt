https://networks.skewed.de/net/dolphins

An undirected social network of frequent associations observed among 62 dolphins (Tursiops) in a community living off Doubtful Sound, New Zealand, from 1994-2001.

D. Lusseau et al., "The bottlenose dolphin community of Doubtful Sound features a large proportion of long-lasting associations." Behavioral Ecology and Sociobiology 54(4), 396-405 (2003)

http://www-personal.umich.edu/~mejn/netdata/
